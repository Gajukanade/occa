Income Tax Compliances

As we are aware that, Tax compliance is inevitable in India and income tax department is also ensuring that each & every eligible person complies with the law well in time. To encourage the people, Income Tax Department has started with system of filing of electronic returns. With help of new electronic filing system, returns are processed immediately and accordingly refunds are credited to Tax Assessee’s Bank account. Gone are the days when people used to wait for IT refunds for 2-3 years. Our specialized team will help you to file Tax Return within time deadline and ensure that return is processed accordingly. With our unique methodology of preparation of IT return, we have been successful to file all returns electronically without any error. This unique methodology of compiling returns will help us when scrutiny assessments are undertaken by the department for such individual clients.

Strategic advice for Tax Compliance in resident country

We are proud to have the NRI clients from almost all states of USA, Europe & other parts of the world. Because of our exposure in international taxation, many NRI clients could understand the exact nature & mechanism of compliance to be done in India as well as in country of residence.

Advice on FEMA compliance & repatriation of funds

We have developed a special wing for NRI clients which is headed by CA Milind Kshirsagar. His expertise in the field of international taxation & Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA), has gained popularity among all our NRI clients. The team provides consultation with reference to applicable FEMA compliances for Non Resident and repatriation of funds to country of residence..

Wealth Related Compliance with reference to properties in India

Many non- resident clients have movable as well as immovable properties in India including gold, silver & precious metals. Appropriate management & record keeping in respect of these properties is essential in overall portfolio management. We render specialized advice in this respect.

Appearance before Tax Authorities

We do appear before Tax Authorities up to level of CIT (Appeals). With our transparent policies of handling the tax assessments over a period of 3 decades, we have been successful in pleading our client’s case.
