CORPORATE ENTITIES

Statutory Audit under Companies Act

Being this is a mandatory compliance under Company Act, we assure transparent professional service to the management. Even though this is yearend activity, our approach of undertaking interim audit for specific areas, has helped the corporate entities to comply with observations well before the financial year close.

Tax Audit & GST Audit

Over the years, tax audit & GST Audit have gained its importance and more responsibility is cast on the assessee as well as Chartered Accountants. Recently, Income Tax Department has shifted to signing & filing of Tax Audit report digitally. Timely & accurate compliance can only save the assessee from any action from revenue. And therefore, both timely & accurate compliance is assured to our clients.

Certification for Foreign Remittances & compliance under FEMA

Now days, being as open economy, it has been very normal & regular to enter into international business transactions. As a result of this, remittances are effected to the foreign country with or without holding of taxes. In order to facilitate proper & legal remittances, various documents & certificates duly signed by Chartered Accountant need to be submitted to Banks. We offer our clients this certification service and ensure that necessary taxes are with-hold by client before he remits the funds to foreign country.

Appearance before Tax Authorities

We do appear before Tax Authorities up to level of CIT (Appeals). With our transparent policies of handling the tax assessments over a period of 3 decades, we have been successful in pleading our client’s case.
